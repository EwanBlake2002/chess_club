About Our Club

Napier Chess Club welcomes players of all levels—from curious beginners to seasoned competitors. Learn, play, and grow in a supportive environment.

Beginners

New to chess? Join our friendly intro sessions and learn at your own pace.

Intermediate

Know the basics? Improve your strategy and sharpen your tactical vision.

Advanced

Play with strong opponents, review games, and elevate your competitive edge.

Weekly Meetings

Every Wednesday, 6:00–9:00 PM

Our weekly meetings include:

  • Casual games and practice sessions
  • Group lessons and strategy discussions
  • Game analysis with experienced players
  • Light refreshments and social time

No equipment needed – we provide chess sets and clocks!
