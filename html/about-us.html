About Napier Chess Club

Where minds meet and strategies unfold

Our Story

What began as a few passionate games in a quiet café corner has become one of the most dynamic societies at Napier University. Napier Chess Club brings together strategists, thinkers, and learners—both casual and competitive—into one welcoming community.

With regular events, tournaments, and friendly meetups, we’ve created a space where every move matters—and every member counts.

Milestones

2018

Club Founded

Napier Chess Club began with a small group of enthusiasts meeting weekly for casual games.

2019

First Tournament

We hosted our first internal blitz tournament — over 30 players competed!

2021

Inter-University Match

Played and won against Stirling and Heriot-Watt in a friendly rapid showdown.

2023

Hybrid Events & Growth

Launched our Discord server and began hosting hybrid online + offline events.
