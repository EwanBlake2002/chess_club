Our Book Recommendations

Perfect guides for beginners wanting to learn the art and science of chess.

Modern Chess Openings

by Nick de Firmian

Modern Chess Openings is the leading reference for serious players, offering thoroughly updated opening theory and insights from recent matches. Ideal for both intermediate players advancing their game and Grandmasters staying current with the latest innovations, this classic guide remains an essential resource.

Download Book (PDF)

Improve Your Chess: Teach Yourself

by William Hartson

Improve Your Chess by William Hartston offers 75 structured lessons with illustrated examples and grandmaster insights to enhance your strategy and piece coordination. Featuring quick tips, self-tests, and online extras, this guide helps players of all levels refine their skills through practical exercises and key principles.

Download Book (PDF)

100 Endgames You Must Know

by Jesus de la Villa

100 Endgames You Must Know by Jesus de la Villa simplifies endgame study by focusing on the most practical, frequently occurring positions that are easy to learn and apply. With clear explanations, helpful diagrams, and practical tests, this updated edition helps players of all levels sharpen their endgame technique efficiently.
